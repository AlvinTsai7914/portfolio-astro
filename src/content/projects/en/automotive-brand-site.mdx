## Overview

A long-term collaboration with a Japanese automotive brand, including ongoing maintenance of the official corporate site, new model launch pages, and interactive campaign experiences.

## My Role

Frontend developer, responsible for:

- Visual updates and new feature development on the corporate site
- Interactive effects on campaign pages
- Iterative collaboration with marketing teams and designers
- Cross-browser compatibility testing

## Technical Highlights

- **Interactive motion**: GSAP for scroll-triggered parallax and timeline animations, Lottie for After Effects integration
- **Long-term maintainability**: Built an extensible component library and style tokens to lower the cost of future feature work
- **Brand consistency**: Strict adherence to brand design guidelines across visual and interaction details
